thumb|Godavari River at Flood time in P.Gannavaram P. Gannavaram (Patha Gannavaram) is a village and mandal in Konaseema district in the state of Andhra Pradesh in India. Patha Gannavaram consists of 18 sub-villages. As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Patha Gannavaram is administrated by a sarpanch (head of village) who is the elected representative of village.
