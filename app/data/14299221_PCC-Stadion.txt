thumb|300px|PCC-Stadion The PCC-Stadion is a football stadium in Homberg (Duisburg), North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. It is the home ground of the women's Bundesliga side MSV Duisburg—continuing the tradition of the FCR 2001 Duisburg—and men's fourth division side VfB Homberg. The stadium has a capacity of 3,000. The main stand has 800 covered seats.
