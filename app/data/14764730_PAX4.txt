Paired box gene 4, also known as PAX4, is a protein which in humans is encoded by the PAX4 gene. == Function == This gene is a member of the paired box (PAX) family of transcription factors. Members of this gene family typically contain a paired box domain, an octapeptide, and a paired-type homeodomain. These genes play critical roles during fetal development and cancer growth.
