Pre-B-cell leukemia transcription factor 2 is a protein that in humans is encoded by the PBX2 gene. == Function == This gene encodes a ubiquitously expressed member of the TALE/PBX homeobox family. It was identified by its similarity to a homeobox gene which is involved in t(1;19) translocation in acute pre-B-cell leukemias. This protein is a transcriptional activator which binds to the TLX1 promoter.
