Parayarukandi Vettath Gangadharan is an Indian film producer and businessman from Kerala. He has produced 22 Malayalam films under his production company Grihalakshmi Productions. As a producer, he has won two National Film Awards, five Kerala State Film Awards, and six Filmfare Awards South among other awards. ==Family== He was born at Calicut in 1943 to Madhavi Sami (1916–1996) and P. V. Sami (1912-1990), a popular businessman and founder of KTC Group of Companies. He is the younger brother of P. V. Chandran, managing editor of Mathrubhumi newspaper, and an industrialist himself. P. V. Gangadharan, as he is known, also serves as the director of Mathrubhumi. He is married to P.V. Sherien, daughter of former Advocate General Ratna Singh.
