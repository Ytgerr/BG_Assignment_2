Poly [ADP-ribose] polymerase 10 is an enzyme that in humans is encoded by the PARP10 gene. Poly(ADP-ribose) polymerases (PARPs), such as PARP10, regulate gene transcription by altering chromatin organization by adding ADP-ribose to histones.
