Palestra Ginnastica Libertas, also known as P.G. Libertas, was an Italian association football club from Florence founded in 1912. The club is short- lived, lasting little more than a decade before merging with fellow Florence club C.S. Firenze in 1926 to form A.C. Fiorentina, which would be one of Serie A's most dominant forces to this day. ==References== Category:ACF Fiorentina Category:Defunct football clubs in Italy Category:Football clubs in Tuscany Category:1912 establishments in Italy Category:1926 disestablishments in Italy
