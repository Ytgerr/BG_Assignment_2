PC Connection, doing business as Connection, is headquartered in Merrimack, New Hampshire. It has more than 2,500 employees and sells more than 300,000 products. The Connection brand includes Connection Business Solutions, Connection MoreDirect Enterprise Solutions, and Connection GovConnection Public Sector Solutions, which provide IT services for small- to medium-sized businesses, enterprises, and public sector markets, respectively. ==History== PC Connection, Inc. was founded in 1982 by Patricia Gallup and David Hall.
