Pakistan Air Force Football Club, commonly known as PAF FC, is a Pakistani professional football club based in Islamabad. It represents the Pakistan Air Force. The club won the National Challenge Cup, after defeating WAPDA 2–1 in the finals of 2018 National Challenge Cup.
