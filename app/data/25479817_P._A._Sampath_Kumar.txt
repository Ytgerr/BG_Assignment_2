P. Antonyraj Sampath Kumar (25 October 1951 – 9 March 2015) was an Indian New Testament scholar who was the President of the Society for Biblical Studies in India, and was the Escande Chair of Asian Christian Studies at Pondicherry UniversityUniversity of Pondicherry - Escande Chair in Asian Christian Studies and was the Chief Warden of the Pondicherry University Hostels.
