Peter Joseph Dranginis (November 13, 1909 – August 5, 1995) was an American football player and coach. He played college football at the Catholic University of America and professionally in the American Football League (AFL) with the Boston Shamrocks and the Pittsburgh Americans. Dranginis served as the co-head football coach at Middlebury College in 1944 with C. W. Laird. Together they compiled a record of 2–1.
