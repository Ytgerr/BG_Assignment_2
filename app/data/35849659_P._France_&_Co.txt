P. France & Co. was a nineteenth-century publishing company based in the early years at 8 The Side, Newcastle: They were responsible for the editing, publishing, printing (and partially for the) selling of the chapbook "Songs of the Bards of the Tyne; A choice selection of original songs, chiefly in the Newcastle Dialect". ==See also== *Geordie dialect words *France's Songs of the Bards of the Tyne - 1850 ==References== ==External links== * Songs of the Bards of the Tyne * Farne archives Category:Music publishing companies of the United Kingdom
