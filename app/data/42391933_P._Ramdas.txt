P. Ramdas (December 1933 – 27 March 2014) was an Indian film director and screenwriter best known for his film Newspaper Boy which was influenced by Italian neorealism. \- In 2007, he was awarded the J. C. Daniel Award, Kerala government's highest honour for contributions to Malayalam cinema.
