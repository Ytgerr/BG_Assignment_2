P'ukru (Quechua for hole, pit, gap in a surface, also spelled Phujro) or Phujru (Aymara for hole or pit in the earth without water, not very deep) is a mountain in the Potosí mountain range in the Bolivian Andes. It is situated in the Potosí Department, Tomás Frías Province, in the south of the Potosí Municipality.
