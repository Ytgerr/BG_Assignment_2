P.C. 369 is a 1987 Indian Malayalam-language film, directed by P. Chandrakumar. The film stars Suresh Gopi, Mukesh, Rohini and Maniyanpilla Raju. The film has musical score by K. P. N. Pillai. ==Cast== *Suresh Gopi as Gopikuttan *Mukesh as Johnny Varghese *Rohini as Sudha *Maniyanpilla Raju as P.C. Damodharan Pilla *Saritha as P.C. Elsamma Mathew *T. G. Ravi as H.C. Cheenkanni Vasu Kurup *Vijayaraghavan as Johnny's brother *Santha Devi as Mandara *Oduvil Unnikrishnan as Adv. Swaminathan / Dr. Viswanathan / Prof. Kashinathan ==Soundtrack== The music was composed by K. P. N. Pillai with lyrics by Chowalloor Krishnankutty and Dr K. Narayanankutty.
