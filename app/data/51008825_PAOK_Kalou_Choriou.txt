PAOK Kalou Choriou () was a Cypriot association football club based in Kalo Chorio, Limassol, located in the Limassol District. It had 1 participation in Cypriot Fourth Division. The team is the theoretical continuitation of Kentro Neotitas Kalou Choriou "Commandaria" and Th.O.I.
