P.I. sometimes referred to as Private Investigator is a Singaporean crime/thriller drama starring Carl Ng, Vanessa Vanderstraaten, Karylle, Kathiravan Kandavelu, Joyce Ng, Najib Soiman and Shane Mardjuki.
