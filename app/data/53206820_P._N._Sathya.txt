P. N. Sathya (died 5 May 2018) was an Indian film director, writer and actor working in Kannada cinema. He was known for writing and directing crime drama films. He debuted with the crime film Majestic (2002) which introduced actor Darshan in the lead role and went on to direct many such films like Don (2003), Daasa (2003), Shashtri (2005), Thangigagi (2006), Sugreeva (2010) and Shivajinagara (2014).
