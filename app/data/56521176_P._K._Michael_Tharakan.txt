P. K. Michael Tharakan is a senior academic, historian and social science researcher from Kerala state in southern India. He is the Chairman of the Kerala Council for Historical Research and has served in several senior academic positions such as the Professor at the social science research institute Institute for Social and Economic Change Bangalore and Vice- Chancellor of Kannur University during 2009-2013.
