thumb|PK Medini P. K. Medini is a revolutionary singer, musician, stage artist, one of the few living freedom fighters of India, a participant of the historical Punnapra-Vayalar uprising, a leader of Communist party of India and a renowned social activist of Kerala. Medini is a recipient of the Kerala Sangeetha Nataka Akademi Award (1999). thumb|P. K.
