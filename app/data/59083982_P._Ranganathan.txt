Padmanabha Ranganathan, more commonly known as P. Ranganathan, (born 25 April 1964) is an Indian former first-class cricketer from the state of Kerala. He played as an opening batsman for Kerala cricket team in Ranji trophy. He is a BCCI level B coach and was appointed as the coach of Kerala team for 2010-11 season in May 2010.Retrieved 17 November 2018.He has scored 1110 runs from 26 matches and has got a wicket.
