P. Yerragonda is a village in Y. Ramavaram Mandal, East Godavari district in the state of Andhra Pradesh in India. == Demographics == India census, This Village had a population of 1244, out of which 245 were male and 999 were female. Population of children below 6 years of age were 4%. The literacy rate of the village is 85%. == References == Category:Villages in Y. Ramavaram mandal
