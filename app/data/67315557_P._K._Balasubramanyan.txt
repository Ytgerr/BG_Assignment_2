P. K. Balasubramanyan (born 28 August 1942) is a former justice of the Supreme Court of India who held the office from August 2004 to August 2007. Prior to this, Balasubramanyan was the chief justice at High Court of Jharkhand. He writes regularly for Outlook Magazine.
