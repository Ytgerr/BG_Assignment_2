PAMO University of Medical Sciences is a private medical university located in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. The university was established in 2017 as the first private medical university in Nigeria. PAMO University of Medical Sciences was founded by the former governor of Rivers State, Dr Sir Peter Odili. In 2017, the university appointed Former Nigerian Head of State, General Abdulsalami Abubakar, as its Chairman of the Governing Council and Board of Trustees. ==Faculties== The university currently have three faculties, namely:- * Faculty of Clinical Sciences * Faculty of Basic Medical Sciences * Faculty of Allied health sciences ==References== Category:Educational institutions established in 2017 Category:2017 establishments in Nigeria Category:Medical schools in Nigeria
