P Saravana Kumar (born 26 August 1989) is an Indian cricketer. He made his Twenty20 debut on 8 November 2021, for Tamil Nadu in the 2021–22 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy, taking two wickets in the match. On 20 November 2021, in the semi- final match of the tournament against Hyderabad, Kumar took his first five- wicket haul in a T20 match, with Tamil Nadu winning by eight wickets to reach the final.
