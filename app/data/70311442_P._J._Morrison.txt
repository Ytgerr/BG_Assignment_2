Peter Joseph 'P. J.' Morrison (born 27 February 1998) is a Scottish footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for current club, Alloa Athletic.PJ Morrison, Motherwell FC. Retrieved 14 March 2021 He was part of the Motherwell Scottish Youth Cup winning side in 2016 and experienced first-team football during loan spells with Clyde, Albion Rovers, Cowdenbeath and Falkirk, where he kept four clean sheets in eight appearances at the start of the 2020–21 season.Motherwell recall goalkeeper PJ Morrison from loan deal, Daily Record, 2 January 2021. Retrieved 14 March 2021 He was called up to the Scotland Under-21 squad for two matches in 2020, but was an unused substitute in two matches.
