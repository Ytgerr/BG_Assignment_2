The PBA Season 47 draft was the 37th edition of the PBA draft. It was an event that allowed teams to take turns selecting amateur basketball players and other eligible players, including half-Filipino foreign players. The league determined the drafting order based on the performance of the member teams from the 2021 season, with the worst team picking first. ==Draft order== In past drafts until 2019, the draft order was determined based on the overall performance of the teams from the previous season. The Philippine Cup final ranking comprised 40% of the points, while the rankings of the Commissioner's and Governors' Cups were 30% each. Since the league only held two tournaments for the 2021 season, only the final rankings of the Philippine Cup (60%) and Governors' Cup (40%) were used to determine the draft order.
