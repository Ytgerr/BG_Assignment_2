P. C. Sanal Kumar (19 June 1949 – 8 November 2014) ) was an Indian satirist who wrote in Malayalam. He was a retired IAS officer in Kerala, India. He was also the District Collector of Kasaragod and Pathanamthitta districts, India. ==Literary career== P. C.
