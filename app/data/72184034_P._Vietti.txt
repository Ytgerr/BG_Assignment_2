P. Vietti (date of birth unknown, died 1917) was a footballer who played for FC Basel in the early 1910s. He played as goalkeeper. Vietti joined Basel's first team for their 1912–13 season under player-coach Ernst Kaltenbach. Vietti played his domestic league debut for the club in the home game in the Landhof on 13 October 1912 as Basel were defeated 1–3 against local rivals Nordstern Basel. He played his last game for the team in the home game on 6 April 1913 as Basel played a 2–2 draw against FC Bern. In his one season with the club Vietti played a total of 12 games for Basel.
