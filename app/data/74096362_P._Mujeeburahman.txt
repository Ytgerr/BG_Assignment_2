P. Mujeeburahman is an Indian organizer, social worker, and speaker, associated with Jamaat Islami Hind in Kerala. He currently serves as the President (Ameer) of Jamaat Islami Hind Kerala State, having been elected for the term 2023–2027. With a career in social and political activism, Mujeeburahman has held various leadership positions within Jamaat-e-Islami Hind and other organizations. == Contributions and activism == P. Mujeeburahman has actively participated in numerous social and political movements, demonstrating his commitment to various causes. He has played significant roles in organizing and leading movements such as the Kinalur strike, anti-Entosulfan strike, rehabilitation of endosulfan victims, Plachimada strike against the Coca-Cola Company, national highway development, Moolambilly strike, anti-monopoly strike, and various other popular struggles.https://www.doolnews.com/tag/p-mujeeb-rahman During his involvement in the Chengara strike for land, Mujeeburahman experienced firsthand the challenges faced by activists, including facing police beatings.
